# N. Decision title in present tense

Date: YYYY-MM-DD

## Status

Proposed | Accepted | Deprecated | Superseded by [N](NNNN-other.md)

## Context

What forces are at play? What problem are we solving? Include
constraints (technical, organizational, regulatory) that shape the
space of acceptable decisions.

## Decision

What did we decide, stated in present-tense active voice. "We will
use X" not "We considered X." If alternatives were rejected, name
them and say briefly why — that's part of the decision's audit trail.

## Consequences

**Positive.** What becomes easier because of this decision.

**Negative.** What becomes harder. What we're explicitly trading off.
Be honest — pretending a decision has no downsides makes the ADR
less useful, not more.
